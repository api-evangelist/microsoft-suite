Most applications used by millions of people every single day are powered by JavaScript/TypeScript. But when it comes to AI, most learning resources and code samples assume you’re working in Python and will leave you trying to stitch scattered tutorials together to build AI into your stack. The JavaScript AI Build-a-thon is a free, hands-on program designed to close that gap.
